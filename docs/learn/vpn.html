What is a VPN?

  • Home
  • Team
  • Support

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) extends a private network across a public one, allowing users to send and receive data securely and anonymously across the internet. It’s an essential tool for privacy, security, and bypassing censorship.

🔐 Key Benefits of a VPN

  • Encrypts internet traffic between your device and the VPN server
  • Masks your IP address to prevent tracking
  • Access region-restricted content (e.g., streaming, censored sites)
  • Enhances security over public Wi-Fi
