Empowering Cybersecurity in Kenya

InfoSecKe is a grassroots-driven initiative committed to raising cybersecurity awareness, promoting ethical hacking practices, and building cyber resilience within Kenyan communities. Our mission is to empower students, professionals, businesses, and organizations to understand and protect themselves from digital threats.

What We Stand For

  • Education First: We believe knowledge is the first line of defense. Through free resources, training, and workshops, we aim to spread critical cyber literacy.
  • Accessibility: We are dedicated to creating open access to security tools and platforms for all, especially underserved regions in Kenya and Africa.
  • Community Engagement: We foster collaboration between hackers, defenders, researchers, and policymakers to build a unified cyber defense front.

Our Objectives

  1. Develop practical cybersecurity skills among Kenyan youth and professionals.
  2. Facilitate secure digital transformations for small businesses and startups.
  3. Create public awareness around common cyber threats and social engineering tactics.
  4. Encourage participation in bug bounty programs and Capture The Flag (CTF) events.
  5. Support open-source security innovation in Kenya and East Africa.
