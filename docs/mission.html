Our Mission

Empowering Cybersecurity in Kenya and Beyond

InfoSecKe is a grassroots cybersecurity movement dedicated to fortifying Kenya’s digital landscape. Our initiative emerged from the growing demand for accessible, practical, and locally relevant cybersecurity education. We operate as a community-driven ecosystem focused on developing homegrown talent, promoting ethical hacking, and fostering innovation in information security practices.

As cyber threats evolve globally, Africa faces a unique set of challenges—from lack of awareness to limited access to tools and training. InfoSecKe aims to bridge this gap by equipping individuals and organizations with the knowledge, skills, and mindset to identify, prevent, and respond to cyber incidents.

What We Believe

  • Cyber Literacy for All: Every Kenyan deserves the knowledge to stay safe online. We make this possible through interactive learning resources, hands-on labs, and awareness campaigns.
  • Community-Led Security: Our strength lies in our people. We champion the collaboration of ethical hackers, security analysts, researchers, students, and tech enthusiasts to create a united defense against cybercrime.
  • Inclusivity and Opportunity: We actively support diversity in cybersecurity and offer mentorship, scholarships, and free tools to underrepresented groups, including women, youth, and rural learners.
  • Open-Source First: We believe in transparency and innovation. Most of our projects are open source, allowing others to contribute, learn, and improve upon them.

Our Core Objectives

  1. Deliver affordable and high-impact cybersecurity education via virtual labs, CTFs, and micro-certifications.
  2. Increase the number of skilled ethical hackers and security engineers across Kenya and East Africa.
  3. Promote responsible disclosure and ethical vulnerability reporting among local bug bounty hunters.
  4. Partner with schools, universities, and local businesses to integrate cybersecurity into everyday operations.
  5. Establish Kenya as a hub for ethical hacking talent and open-source cyber tools development.

Our Strategic Initiatives

  • Cyber Labs & Range: An interactive cyber range that simulates real-world attacks to prepare learners for red team/blue team roles.
  • Mentorship & Career Pathways: Guided tracks for penetration testers, SOC analysts, DevSecOps engineers, and more.
  • Cyber Threat Reports: Monthly analysis of emerging threats, CVEs, and localized security incidents.
  • Research & Development: Supporting homegrown tools like VulnHunter, KasauXSS, and our Auto-Lab Generator for ethical hacking practice.
  • National Awareness Campaigns: Offline and online outreach programs targeting digital safety for students, families, and SMEs.

Our Vision

We envision a digitally sovereign Kenya where cybersecurity is not a privilege but a basic right—where every citizen, institution, and enterprise has access to the tools and training necessary to thrive in a connected world.
