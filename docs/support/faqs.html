🔧 Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions and troubleshooting help for InfoSecKe tools, account issues, and participation in events.

🔐 How do I reset my account password?
Go to the password reset page, enter your email, and follow the link sent to your inbox. Make sure to check your spam folder.
📧 I'm not receiving newsletter or email confirmations
Ensure emails from @infosecke.org are not blocked or sent to spam. Add us to your safe sender list and verify that your email is correctly listed in your profile.
🛠 Why won't the vulnerability scanner run on my machine?
Make sure you're using Linux or WSL, have the latest version of Go or Python installed, and have granted the tool permissions (e.g., with chmod +x). Visit our VulnHunter docs for full instructions.
🎮 How do I join a CTF or bug bounty?
Visit the Events page, find an active challenge, and click “Register.” For bounties, check our partners or the Hall of Fame for targets accepting submissions.
💬 Can I get mentorship or join local meetups?
Yes! Head to the Community page and apply for mentorship or join one of our Telegram/Discord regional meetups in Kenya.
⚙️ Where can I report a bug or feature request?
Submit issues via our contact form or file a GitHub issue on our open-source repos.
📂 How can I contribute to InfoSecKe’s tools?
We love contributors! Start by checking out our Tools page, clone a repo, and submit PRs. Make sure to follow the contribution guidelines in each project.
