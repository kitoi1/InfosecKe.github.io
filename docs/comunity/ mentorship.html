The InfoSecKe Mentorship Program connects aspiring cybersecurity professionals with seasoned mentors who offer guidance, resources, and industry insight to help them grow and succeed.

🎯 Who Can Apply?

  • University and college students interested in cybersecurity.
  • Beginners looking to break into ethical hacking, GRC, or DFIR.
  • Junior professionals seeking structured learning support.

🧑‍🏫 What Mentors Help With

Career Guidance

Plan your path: Bug Bounty? SOC Analyst? Red Team? Mentors help you find your lane.

Learning Tracks

Get access to curated learning roadmaps and practical assignments based on your interests.

Soft Skills & Confidence

Improve presentation skills, report writing, CV optimization, and more.

📅 Program Structure

  • ✅ Duration: 3 months (renewable upon evaluation)
  • ✅ Weekly check-ins via Google Meet, Zoom, or Discord
  • ✅ Assignments & skill assessments every 2 weeks
  • ✅ Final capstone project & certification
